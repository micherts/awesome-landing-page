About Us

Who we are

HospoSure brings together some of the best hospitality minds in Australia. Our founders combine over 50 years of world-class experience mixing technological, operational, and financial skills into a user-friendly solution. We are proudly designed and manufactured in Australia by Aussies.

What we do

We have developed an easy-to-use cloud-based programme that provides both cafe entrepreneurs who are planning to open and those who are currently open for business with crucial training into how a cafe operation should function to ensure operational sustainability.

Why we do it

Up to 70% of cafes fail in the first year of business, and cafe leases are now changing hands in as little as a year. Nobody likes scare tactics, but the reality is without accurate business modelling and financial and operational understanding, your chances of success in the hospitality and cafe industry are slim.

How we do it

Our cafe planning software is the result of two years of development, extensive product testing, and real-life user case studies. It combines expert coaching notes with high-level technology and flawless reports.

What does this mean for you?

Superb user experience, extensive lightning-fast results, vast compound growth of cafe operational knowledge, personal risk reduction, and increased chances of cafe success.

What's next?

Our in-house technology and operations teams are exploring the introduction of integrational technologies to enable 360-degree pre-opening education to ongoing cafe operations, enabling total visibility into operations for the life of your cafe.
